Frequently asked

Do you charge per user?

No. Plans are scoped on monthly invoice volume. AP clerks, approvers, and viewers are unlimited within the plan.

What counts as an invoice?

One AP item created in Clearledgr. Duplicate inbound emails for the same invoice key consolidate into one item, so they don't count twice.

Can I trial Clearledgr?

Yes. Starter and Growth offer 14-day no-card trials. Reach out for an Enterprise trial scope.

How do you bill?

Monthly via Stripe (when paid plans go live). Annual contracts available with a 12% discount.
