How we handle your data

Authentication

Sign in with Google, Microsoft, or email/password. Passwords are bcrypt-hashed. Session cookies are HttpOnly with SameSite-Lax. CSRF tokens are validated on every mutating request.

Role-based access

Seven hierarchical roles — Owner, CFO, Financial Controller, AP Manager, AP Clerk, Approver, Read-only. Tenant isolation is enforced on every query by organization_id; column-whitelist guards prevent injection on dynamic UPDATE paths.

Encryption

TLS 1.2+ enforced at ingress with HSTS. AES-256 at rest in the managed Postgres. Sensitive fields — bank details, ERP credentials, OAuth refresh tokens — are encrypted with Fernet on top of the database encryption, derived from a key never touched by the LLM.

Audit logging

Every state transition writes an append-only row to audit_events. Database triggers reject any UPDATE or DELETE on the audit table. Idempotency keys prevent duplicate event recording.

Rate limiting + CSRF

Production traffic is rate-limited at the middleware layer with Redis-backed buckets; CSRF tokens are required on every cookie-authenticated mutation.

Incident response

Severity tiers, on-call rotation, 72-hour breach notification SLA. Customers receive an RCA within one week of any incident touching their data.

Compliance roadmap

Today. Controls equivalent to SOC2 are implemented and documented in our security packet, with file:line citations into the source. We share the packet under NDA on request.

Next. SOC2 Type 1 audit (~6 weeks once auditor is engaged), then a 6-month observation window for Type 2.

Ongoing. Dependabot watches dependencies weekly. Sentry surfaces errors with PII scrubbing applied at the SDK boundary. Sub-processors are documented and customers receive 30 days' notice before any change.
